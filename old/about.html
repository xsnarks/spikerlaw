About

Rachel B. Spiker was born and raised in Martinsburg, West Virginia. She attended Shepherd College, graduating summa cum laude in 2001 with a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology and History. She graduated from Emory University School of Law in 2004. After law school , she returned to her hometown, and since then, has accumulated over 10 years of experience in real estate law, estate law, and business organizations law. Rachel is licensed in Maryland and West Virginia, and is a member of the Eastern Panhandle Bar Association and the West Virginia State Bar Association. Outside of the office, she enjoys volunteering with various animal rescues and working on improving animal welfare.
