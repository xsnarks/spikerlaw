Estate Planning

Estate planning can be a very stressful and uncomfortable undertaking. My goal is to make the process as simple and painless as possible. During your first appointment, we will discuss your goals and the best way to achieve them. After your documents are drafted, we'll set up a second appointment, during which, we'll review and execute your documents. Contact me at 304-886-9541 or spiker@spikerlaw.com to discuss your estate planning needs.


Additionally, the death of a loved one can be a very confusing and overwhelming time. Should you need assistance with the probate process, please contact me.
